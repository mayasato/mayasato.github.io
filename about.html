Maya Sato is a Chicago-based artist, working primarily in drawing, printmaking, and design. She graduated from DePaul University with a degree in Art and Design. She currently works as a professional screenprinter and freelance artist in Chicago, with pieces purchased and shown nationally. Her work is informed by exposure to a wide range of cultures and worldviews, and focuses primarily on the idea of home.
