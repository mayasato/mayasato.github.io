I am an aspiring studio artist and graphic designer based in Chicago. I am a normal human with abnormal thoughts that manifest as creativity. I am currently working toward degrees in Studio Arts and Graphic Design, and I have various professional and non-professional experience in both (you can view that stuff in my resume). Dry/absurd humor is important to me. I tend to make things that are either funny and absurd, or serious and intellectual. There is no in-between. My portfolio includes both commercial and personal projects and exhibits my exploration of a range of mediums.
